# U-17 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`

U-17 is a compensation-for-risk characteristic inside an already liquid and
investable universe. It is not U-06's temporary volume-share shock with muted
price response, U-08's discrete membership admission, U-09's low residual
volatility quality, U-10's price/volume trend confirmation, U-15's taker-buy
absorption or U-16's correlation breakdown. It requires neither a price move,
market state, calendar event nor order-flow imbalance at design time.

The mechanism can fail if lower activity is stale attention or terminal decay,
if the Top-15 liquidity range is economically too narrow, if a future proxy
confounds price or age, if the characteristic is unstable, or if impact and
capacity costs consume the premium. This review establishes design-level
independence only; it provides no evidence of feasibility or profitability.
