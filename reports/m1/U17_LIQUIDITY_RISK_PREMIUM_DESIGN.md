# U-17 Cross-Sectional Liquidity-Risk Premium Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U17-CROSS-SECTIONAL-LIQUIDITY-RISK-PREMIUM`
- Hypothesis hash: `19b0e7eaa4d9f8432b733d3660a76226c9e3351674d21a819adf266515261fea`
- Design hash: `ad3b6a4cabc61bd41890684ccf30b8ee4ae3a2811fcc4dd08abcf45a4bf420fe`

The hypothesis is a persistent characteristic mechanism. Within the already
investable point-in-time Top-15 universe, an asset that remains less liquid
than its active peers may expose holders to marginal immediacy, crowded-exit
and capital-capacity risk. Investors may require compensation for bearing that
risk, so a cross-sectional liquidity premium may accrue only after the next
eligible open.

This design reads no data or outcome and selects no timeframe, history length,
liquidity proxy, rank estimator, persistence rule, cohort, threshold, horizon,
cost Gate or strategy rule. It does not assume that low liquidity is profitable;
that proposition must survive a separately frozen, reviewed Paper protocol.
OOS remains sealed.
