# U-18 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`

U-18 concerns persistent asymmetry of the asset-specific return distribution
and compensation for bearing its downside tail. It is not U-04 or M1G
single-event reversal, U-07 market-stress resilience, U-09 low total residual
volatility, U-11 positive/negative beta capture, U-14 intrabar downside
rejection, or U-17 trading-liquidity risk. No price-direction event is selected
at design time.

The mechanism can fail through sparse tails, permanent information losses,
common-component distortion, lifecycle discontinuities, nonstationarity,
uncompensated crashes or execution costs. This establishes design-level
independence only and provides no evidence of feasibility or profitability.
