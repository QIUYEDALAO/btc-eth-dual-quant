# U-11 Asymmetric Market-Capture Quality-Persistence Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U11-CROSS-SECTIONAL-ASYMMETRIC-MARKET-CAPTURE-QUALITY-PERSISTENCE`
- Hypothesis hash: `1c9ef854245c3f46000712894392b5b97a865013355c4553e372c6d168f63e74`

Within the exact point-in-time active liquid universe, an asset that
persistently participates in completed positive common-market moves while
preserving capital during completed negative common-market moves may have a
more stable demand base, lower forced-selling sensitivity and broader capital
sponsorship. That asymmetric quality may persist after the next eligible open
without requiring an absolute market-direction forecast.

This design makes no efficacy claim. Timeframe, common component, positive and
negative state definitions, capture estimators, persistence, sample Gates,
candidate thresholds, horizons, clustering, costs and all trading rules remain
unresolved for a separate outcome-blind protocol. No public data, event, path,
return or OOS value was read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
