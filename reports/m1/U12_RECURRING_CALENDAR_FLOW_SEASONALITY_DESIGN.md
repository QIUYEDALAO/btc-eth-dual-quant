# U-12 Recurring UTC Calendar-Flow Seasonality Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`

- Hypothesis hash: `5942e15d4c4dde246979ef980f0fc3c2a3f545e9e6717159355ee4312c87f54d`

Within the exact point-in-time active liquid universe, an asset-specific
relative-return pattern that repeats in the same predeclared UTC calendar state
across disjoint completed historical subperiods may reflect recurring regional
participation, settlement, treasury or mandate flows. That flow may recur after
the next eligible open without assuming absolute market direction.

This design makes no efficacy claim. Calendar-state family and granularity,
timeframe, common component, history, subperiods, estimator, persistence,
multiple-testing control, sample Gates, candidate thresholds, horizons,
clustering, costs and all trading rules remain unresolved for a separate
outcome-blind protocol. No public data, event, path, return or OOS value was
read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
