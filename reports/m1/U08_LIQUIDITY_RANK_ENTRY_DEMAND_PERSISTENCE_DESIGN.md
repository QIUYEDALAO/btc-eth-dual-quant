# U-08 Liquidity-Rank Entry Demand-Persistence Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U08-POINT-IN-TIME-LIQUIDITY-RANK-ENTRY-DEMAND-PERSISTENCE`
- Hypothesis hash: `5d091b72b4ac77669bfe26659d7a95554bb182ba5c138ca20959e93a2e2345f6`

An asset newly entering the frozen point-in-time monthly Top-15 liquidity
membership may become visible or eligible to a broader set of liquidity-aware
participants. Because those participants need not rebalance simultaneously,
the broadened demand may persist after membership becomes effective.

The design uses only prior-complete membership authority and makes no claim
about efficacy. Timeframe, reference open, horizons, clustering, sample Gates,
cost Gate and every trading rule remain unresolved for a separate outcome-blind
protocol. No public data, event, path, return or OOS value was read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
