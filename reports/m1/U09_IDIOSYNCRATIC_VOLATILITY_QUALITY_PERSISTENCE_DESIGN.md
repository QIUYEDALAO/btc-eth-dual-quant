# U-09 Idiosyncratic-Volatility Quality-Persistence Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U09-CROSS-SECTIONAL-IDIOSYNCRATIC-VOLATILITY-QUALITY-PERSISTENCE`
- Hypothesis hash: `cb91d77d11b0f877007848330df7e05e1d4e9257142fce4d339edefe75e14c92`

Within the exact point-in-time active liquid universe, an asset whose completed
price path exhibits persistently lower asset-specific variability after the
contemporaneous common market component is removed may reflect deeper
liquidity, lower disagreement and broader capital eligibility. Those quality
characteristics may persist after the next eligible open.

The design makes no claim that low variability guarantees positive absolute
returns. Timeframe, common-factor estimator, volatility estimator, persistence
definition, thresholds, horizons, clustering, sample Gates, cost Gate and every
trading rule remain unresolved for a separate outcome-blind protocol. No public
data, event, path, return or OOS value was read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
