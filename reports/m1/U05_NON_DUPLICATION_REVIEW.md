# U-05 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`
- Candidate: `U05-CROSS-SECTIONAL-BREADTH-DEMAND-PERSISTENCE`

U-05 studies distributed participation in a common cross-sectional move. It is
not derived from the sign or magnitude of the failed U-04 observation.

- U-04 isolates one asset's negative residual and expects reversal; U-05
  requires distributed common participation and makes no residual claim.
- M1C selects one BTC/ETH long-horizon relative winner; U-05 concerns the exact
  active Top-15 cross-section and does not select a winner.
- M1E studies compression followed by breakout; U-05 has no compression state.
- M1G studies an absolute single-asset panic; U-05 requires broad participation.
- M1H uses settled funding; U-05 is price/breadth-only spot research.
- M1A uses the SMA/Donchian/ATR bundle; every part of that bundle remains
  prohibited.

Removing any of these boundaries invalidates design-level non-duplication.
