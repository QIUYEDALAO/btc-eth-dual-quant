- Status: `pass_design_level`
- Candidate: `U15-CROSS-SECTIONAL-TAKER-BUY-ABSORPTION-PERSISTENCE`

U-15 is distinguished by the joint state of trade-side participation and weak
contemporaneous price impact. It does not reuse U-04 residual reversal, U-05
breadth, U-06 total quote-volume share, U-07 stress resilience, U-08 membership
entry, U-09 volatility quality, U-10 trend confirmation, U-11 beta asymmetry,
U-12 calendar flow, U-13 lagged diffusion or U-14 wick/close-location rejection.

The economic claim is specifically that urgent buyer-initiated flow can be
temporarily absorbed by finite passive sell inventory. Total volume without
taker-side identity is insufficient, and taker-buy participation without muted
relative price response is insufficient. No prior candidate outcome, failed
Gate, sign, path, concentration, censor pattern or defect selected this design.

This is a design-level independence finding, not evidence that the mechanism
exists or is profitable. The next protocol must qualify taker-buy field
authority before authorizing any data qualification or event observation.
