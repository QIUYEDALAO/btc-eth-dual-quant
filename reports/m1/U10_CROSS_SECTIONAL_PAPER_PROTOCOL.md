- Status: `frozen_before_result_pending_exact_head_review`
- Candidate: `U10-CROSS-SECTIONAL-VOLUME-CONFIRMED-RELATIVE-TREND-CONTINUATION`

At each completed UTC day, the protocol uses a 7-day asset return relative to
the active-member median and quote-volume share from complete 5m-derived days.
A candidate must have relative trend of at least 3%, rank in the highest
cross-sectional quartile, have a recent-3-day versus prior-21-day median share
ratio of at least 1.25, rank in that highest quartile, and exceed its baseline
share on each of the recent three days. One deterministic representative is
retained, then all events are clustered in a 72h connected window.

Before any price scan, calendar and membership metadata must prove at least 400
theoretical constant-membership 72h episodes, above the frozen sample Gate 90.
The strict next expected 5m open begins 1/2/4/8/12/24/48/72h diagnostics.

Paper Gates require 90 complete IS episodes, projected 120 full and 30 sealed-
OOS episodes, broad year/symbol/month coverage, median 72h relative and absolute
displacement of at least 1.80%, at least 60% positive relative continuation and
zero authority/order mismatches.

This protocol creates no fill, position, equity or formal return. It opens no
OOS and authorizes only an exact-head independent review.
