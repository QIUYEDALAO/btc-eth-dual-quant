# U-13 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`

U-13 studies temporal propagation of a completed common positive shock across
assets with a preregistered prior-only lag relationship. It is not:

- U-04 contemporaneous negative-residual reversal;
- U-05 broad demand persistence or U-07 stress-relative strength;
- U-06 volume-share absorption or U-10 price/volume trend;
- U-08 membership-entry demand, U-09 low residual volatility, U-11 asymmetric
  market capture, or U-12 recurring calendar flow;
- M1C winner rotation, M1E breakout, M1G panic reversal, M1H funding crowding,
  or the M1A indicator bundle.

The future protocol must define lag estimation and permanent-weakness exclusion
before data access. It may not infer those rules from any prior result.
