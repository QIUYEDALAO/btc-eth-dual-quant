# U-19 Idiosyncratic Volatility-of-Volatility Risk Premium Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U19-CROSS-SECTIONAL-IDIOSYNCRATIC-VOLATILITY-OF-VOLATILITY-RISK-PREMIUM`
- Hypothesis hash: `6b3cd19be7fedab64bfc43ad6c667cdbe774d58994073446a340cf7f55217c52`
- Design hash: `89cd46e632d5d890b5c2b13c71a63642ab1ed5a2167904df0f722a5d4279fe80`

After removing the completed active-peer common component, persistent
variability of an asset's own volatility may impose unstable risk-budget,
hedging and liquidity-capital demands. Investors may require compensation for
bearing that uncertainty, with any premium beginning no earlier than the next
eligible open.

This design reads no data or outcome and selects no timeframe, history,
volatility estimator, volatility-of-volatility estimator, persistence rule,
threshold, horizon, cost Gate or strategy rule. It does not assume the premium
exists; a separately frozen Paper protocol must test the proposition. OOS
remains sealed.
