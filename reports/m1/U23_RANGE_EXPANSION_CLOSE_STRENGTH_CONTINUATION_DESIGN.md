# U-23 Range-Expansion Close-Strength Continuation Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_and_synthetic_core_feasibility_only`
- Candidate: `U23-CROSS-SECTIONAL-RANGE-EXPANSION-CLOSE-STRENGTH-CONTINUATION`
- Hypothesis hash: `6c099769b5fb89d7faf032d68c114540a6b93f8546bbedee26c2e9397e98627f`
- Design hash: `351ddd25e3c8708e55a21e81921d104f4c360b65966945b846fa40ba1e96e6e0`

When an active member's completed high-low auction range expands unusually
relative to its own completed history, the completed close remains near that
range's high, and its displacement is positive relative to every complete
active peer cross section, the geometry may reveal aggressive demand and
incomplete price discovery. Relative strength may therefore persist beginning
no earlier than the next eligible open.

This design reads no public data or outcome and selects no timeframe, history,
range baseline, close-location threshold, peer estimator, horizon, cost Gate
or strategy rule. The exact statistic core must pass a synthetic-only
complexity check before the later protocol is frozen. OOS remains sealed.
