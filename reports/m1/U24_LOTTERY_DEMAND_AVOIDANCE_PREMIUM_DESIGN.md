# U-24 Lottery-Demand Avoidance Premium Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_and_synthetic_core_feasibility_only`

- Design hash: `c99f7002b006d635d5c03936055e8d4bc498ace02b45551af52cdf80dd14d0c1`

Some investors may pay too much for assets offering rare, asset-specific
extreme upside. After removing the completed active-peer common component, an
asset whose completed return distribution persistently offers less right-tail
lottery payoff than its complete point-in-time peers may avoid that preference-
driven overpricing. A relative premium may therefore accrue no earlier than the
next eligible open.

This design reads no public data or outcome and selects no timeframe, history,
common estimator, shape statistic, rank, persistence rule, horizon, cost Gate
or strategy rule. The exact statistic core must pass a synthetic-only
complexity check before a later protocol is frozen. OOS remains sealed.
