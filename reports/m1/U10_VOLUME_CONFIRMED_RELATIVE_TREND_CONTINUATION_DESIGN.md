# U-10 Volume-Confirmed Relative-Trend Continuation Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`

- Hypothesis hash: `5fc3529f7afa3df2344517f3bd567c5b3bf89a23bc0e071ff853bc80e50ec536`

Within the exact point-in-time active liquid universe, positive asset-specific
relative price trend accompanied by persistent expansion in cross-sectional
quote-volume share may indicate scalable demand rather than a thinly traded
price move. Gradual information diffusion and participant rebalancing may allow
that relative trend to continue after the next eligible open.

The design makes no efficacy claim. Timeframe, common component, trend and
volume-share estimators, persistence rule, thresholds, horizons, clustering,
sample/cost Gates and all trading rules remain unresolved for a separate
outcome-blind protocol. No public data, event, path, return or OOS value was read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
