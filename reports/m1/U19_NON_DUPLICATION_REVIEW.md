# U-19 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`

U-19 concerns the instability of asset-specific risk itself and compensation
for bearing unpredictable risk-budget and hedging demand. It is not U-09's low
level of idiosyncratic volatility, U-18's downside-tail asymmetry, U-11's
positive/negative beta capture, U-17's trading liquidity characteristic, or a
price-direction, panic, trend, absorption or correlation-breakdown event.

The mechanism can fail through estimator noise, overlap dependence, common-
component distortion, lifecycle discontinuities, nonstationarity,
uncompensated regime changes or execution costs. This is design-level
independence only and provides no feasibility or profitability evidence.
