# U-13 Common-Shock Lagged Diffusion Design

- Status: `economic_hypothesis_pass_protocol_design_only`
- Candidate: `U13-CROSS-SECTIONAL-COMMON-SHOCK-LAGGED-DIFFUSION`
- Hypothesis hash: `39f1848fe195be8e6c406a5e21b734ce9c469a9df5c08eaa0a70de348988db5c`

Within the exact point-in-time active liquid universe, a completed broad
positive information or capital-flow shock may be absorbed at different speeds
across assets. An asset with a stable, prior-only tendency to respond with delay
rather than permanent weakness may continue catching up after the next eligible
open as attention, liquidity and capital rotate through a fragmented market.

This design makes no efficacy claim. Timeframe, common-shock definition,
breadth, common component, lag estimator, history, persistence, permanent-
weakness exclusion, sample Gates, thresholds, horizons, clustering, costs and
all trading rules remain unresolved for a separate outcome-blind protocol. No
public data, event, path, return or OOS value was read.

No strategy, backtest, API/trading, `execution/live` or M2 is authorized.
