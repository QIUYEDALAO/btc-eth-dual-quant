# U-07 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`
- Candidate: `U07-CROSS-SECTIONAL-MARKET-STRESS-RELATIVE-STRENGTH-CONTINUATION`

U-07 studies state-conditioned relative resilience during broad contemporaneous
market stress. It is not derived from any prior candidate result.

- U-04 selects negative asset-specific residuals and expects reversal; U-07
  selects relative resilience during a separately required broad stress state
  and hypothesizes continuation.
- U-05 concerns distributed positive common demand; U-07 requires broad selling
  pressure plus one asset-specific resilience channel.
- U-06 requires a quote-volume-share/price disagreement; U-07 uses no quote-volume signal.
- M1C is unconditional long-cycle BTC/ETH winner rotation; U-07 is a broad
  point-in-time Top-15 stress-state mechanism and may not reuse M1C's ranking rule.
- M1E compression/breakout, M1G absolute panic, M1H settled funding and M1A's
  SMA/Donchian/ATR bundle are not reused.

Removing any boundary invalidates design-level non-duplication.
