# U-14 Non-Duplication Review

- Status: `pass_design_level`

U-14 uses completed intraperiod auction geometry—downside excursion followed by
a high close-location under common selling pressure—as evidence of demand
absorption. It is not U-04 close-to-close residual reversal, U-06 volume-share
absorption, U-07 completed-return relative strength, U-09 low idiosyncratic
volatility, U-10 trend/volume confirmation, U-11 asymmetric capture, U-12
calendar flow or U-13 delayed common-shock diffusion.

Future protocol rules may not collapse the candidate to absolute return,
low volatility, a volume signal, a prior failed candidate or a known-result
exception. Such a change would fail this candidate identity.
