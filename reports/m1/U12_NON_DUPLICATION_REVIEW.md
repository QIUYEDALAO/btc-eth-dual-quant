and requires persistence across disjoint prior subperiods. It is not U-04
residual reversal, U-05 breadth, U-06 volume absorption, U-07 stress resilience,
U-08 membership entry, U-09 low idiosyncratic volatility, U-10 price/volume
trend or U-11 asymmetric market capture.

funding and the M1A indicator bundle. U-11's invalid attempt and execution
defect, and every prior event sign, measured path, failed Gate, censor pattern
or protocol defect, were excluded from mechanism selection.
