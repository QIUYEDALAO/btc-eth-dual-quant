# U-05 Cross-Sectional Research Mainline

## Objective

Research one independent, outcome-blind point-in-time cross-sectional spot
long/cash candidate through preregistered Paper, fixed-rule IS and unique OOS
validation. Stop at every failed Gate and before each OOS, M2 or trading
permission escalation.

U-04 remains immutable `failed_feasibility`. Its observed event direction and
recovery measurements are not inputs to U-05 mechanism or rule selection.

## Candidate Route

The design-stage default is `U05-CROSS-SECTIONAL-BREADTH-DEMAND-PERSISTENCE`:
a broad, simultaneous participation of the exact active liquid cross-section
may represent diversified capital demand that persists beyond a completed
observation. This is a breadth/common-demand mechanism, not U-04 residual
inversion, M1C winner rotation, M1E compression breakout, M1G panic reversal,
M1H funding crowding or M1A indicator trend.

The design stage selects no timeframe, breadth fraction, return threshold,
holding period, target, stop, position size or performance Gate. Those remain
unresolved until the separately frozen protocol and fixed-rule stages.

## Execution Discipline

- Each complete stage uses one local `codex/` branch, one final local commit and
  one unified acceptance run.
- No push, PR or GitHub Actions unless explicitly requested.
- Passing stages advance automatically. Candidate failure, evidence drift or
  framework mismatch stops fail closed.
- Opening OOS, M2, dry-run, API or live always pauses for explicit permission.
- Production data/evidence are immutable; no current-member backfill, future
  data, same-close fill, synthetic fill or lifecycle rewrite is permitted.
- A failed candidate is append-only and cannot be repaired by parameter changes.
